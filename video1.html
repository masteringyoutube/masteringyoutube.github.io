What is Live Streaming and why should you bother with it?

So What is Live Streaming?

Streaming is a term that makes reference to the hearing or watching videos without the requirement of downloading them in full before watching or hearing them.

This is achieved thanks to fragments sent sequentially through the web referring to Internet content delivered in real-time, as events happen, much like live television broadcasts.

This used to be known on the internet as Streaming Media, which is a complete term for the transmission of video or audio.

When the transmission is live it is known as live Streaming, another quick example we know of is the Live Streaming made when Radio is being transmitted through the web.

So in simple terms, Live Streaming is Recording Yourself or your Event LIVE through the web, in REAL TIME. Pretty similar to a Webinar.

What are its benefits?

Organically generate bigger audiences: Live streaming Video has the ability to organically generate bigger audiences. Your events can surpass any physical limitations with unlimited broadcasting range.

No regional restrictions: It enables anyone to watch anywhere with no regional restrictions. The live content on the web is accessible from anywhere worldwide with no strings attached.

Raises brand awareness: Statistics show consumers are more likely to view a branded video, increasing chances that your video will be seen.

Become the “Authority”: The goal of online marketing is to become the “authority” in your niche or field thus increasing customer awareness, branding and lead generation and live video streaming makes it possible.

In SectionCreate Buzz: Live Streaming creates a buzz about your company or event on the web: this is highly effective for spreading the word about a new product or service.

Urgent entity: Streaming content live for a one time only event makes your stream become a rare and urgent entity: If your stream is only available at one moment in time its urgency can create quite a stir on the web.

Stand out from the crowd: It is an opportunity for new revenue and online interaction which makes you stand out from the crowd because it’s not widely used by many organizations.

Get more: Go live and do more, show more, and get more.
