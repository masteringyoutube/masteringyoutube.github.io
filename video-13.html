Quick-Top-Ranking Tricks By using Product Launches

What if I can show you a way where you can rank YouTube Live Videos in literally seconds, with keywords that will become highly searched money keywords in a specific day you can know in advance?

Yeah, that is absolutely possible with Product Launches.

In simple terms, a Product Launch, is the debut of a product into the market. A product launch is the point at which potential buyers first have access to a new product entering into the market. In this case, the online market.

With that said, the name of the product to be launched can be considered as a potential money keyword, and besides that, the launch is not happening just yet, that money keyword has absolutely no competition, because no other people have created a YouTube Video about it yet.

Making you the first one.

But still, it’s important for you to notice in advance if there already is a YouTube Video ranking in the top of Google with the money keyword of the product launch you are about to schedule in your coming stream event.

If not, this is your golden chance to be the first one and claim that top stop of Google and YouTube before the launch day.

If you Google Product Launch Calendar, you will find several options to pick from.

Muncheye.com, is a very popular Product Launch Notification Page, where top online marketers post their coming product launches.

Jvnewswatch.com, is another very popular Product Launch listing website.

There are 3 different things you will be looking for.

First of all, you will be looking for what is known as the JV Page, where you will be able to get all the information about the launch coming up. Make sure you pick a launch that will come several days after the date you are searching for.

Secondly, you will need to find the Product Name, which is the money keyword you will use to create your live stream.

Now will be a good time to do some basic research and make sure there are no other videos ranking with the product name you are about to use.

If you see a YouTube video ranking with that product name followed with the word “Review”. To make it easy for you, it will be better for you look for another product to launch.

And thirdly, you will set up everything needed to get your affiliate link, as well as advertising tools you can use to promote that product launch and make commissions for every sale generated for your advertising efforts.

There is no point on ranking a video, and getting a lot of people to watch it, and not giving them the chance to buy the product, and generating an affiliate commission for you, because of it.

Now it’s time to create your Live Stream for it.

Go to your “creator studio” and click on the “live streaming” tab and then on “event”.

Click on “new live event”.

Enter the name of your product in the “title”.

For this we advise you to use the Product Name, but another awesome idea is to include the word “Review” at the end of it, as it is something a lot of people search for, before deciding to buy the product.

For the Date, you have 2 options.

Option 1: you can schedule a live stream to go live on the launch date and time. That live stream invitation will be ranked in the top as a reminder, and will stay there, until you go live together with the Product Launch. You can add cards to that Stream Invitation inside the Events dashboard.

Option 2: you can have a live stream Today and Now, giving your honest review about the product, and also letting people know the date when the product will be available for purchase.

Once you finish that live stream, that live stream will be ranked in the top as a video, and will stay there too. So, people can have a review of the product right away. And also on the launch day, you can have that video already ranked there, before your competitors.

Either way works awesome. It depends on what you prefer.

For the description, it is very important to use the exact money keyword you used in the title, but also don’t forget to include your affiliate link. So, viewers can click on it, buy the product, and you make a commission for every sale.

For the “tags”, just add your money keyword exactly, then click on “go live now”.

Awesome.

Go to Google and YouTube and check how your Live Stream is ranking on top.

As you can see, the video is ranked on top in Google, and in YouTube.
