Quick-Top-Ranking Tricks By using Product Names

Branding a product is a challenging task, but the rewards are amazing when branding is done right.

Choosing a name for a product is a lot like choosing a name for your business: it has to be a reflection of what it offers while being catchy and memorable at the same time.

In our last video, we talked about the benefits of using your own name and the name of your business to rank on top of Google and YouTube results.

The greater benefits we mentioned were how using your unique name could be used to rank on top and how ranking on top would help your unique name to become a recognizable brand in itself.

And the good news is that you can apply the same trick using the name of a product. And the more unique and catchy the name of your product is, the lesser the keyword competition will be, letting you to reach the top spot again!

Added benefits to the ones mentioned before include:

-A truly unique product name for your product has not likely been invented except by you, so the first time that your product pops up on YouTube and Google will mark its birth, so every time that the same keyword is used in other places it will be because of your product!

-You can use a product name to describe an existing product if you are promoting a product that has been released before. The combination of the product name plus your made up name will give it a further push towards the top!

Of course, creating a product name might need a little optimization on your part, so we recommend you to:

-Make it highly readable, writable and easy to say

-Make it unique!

-Make it as short as possible

-It should remind people of an idea or an emotion

-It should include a real word when possible, so you can blend real words with made up ones!
