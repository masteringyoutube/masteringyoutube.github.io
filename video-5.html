Verifying your YouTube Channel Account

It has been great so far, as you have advanced to the point where you already created your YouTube account and set up your own dedicated channel. We know that you feel more than eager to start taking over the live streaming world but there is one last step that you have to take care of.

You see, out of the box, your YouTube account and by extension, your YouTube channel, are bound with some limitations set by Google policies that are intended to protect all the people that use their many services, and YouTube is one of the most popular but also one of the most abused ones.

So in order to avoid having to deal with mischievous users, YouTube requires people creating accounts and channels to verify them. Verifying your YouTube account is a very simple process that will not eat up more than 5 minutes of your time and has its benefits.

First of all, it will put your community and copyright status out of danger, because it will present you to consumers and copyright holders as a verified and legitimate user, which also shows Google that you are complying with their guidelines as well.

Secondly, certain limitations to your account will be lifted, and after verifying your account you will be able to:

-Upload videos longer than 15 minutes, which you would not be able to do while unverified

-To get funded by fans and users in general, which is great for kick-starting ideas that might be out of your budget or capacity for audience outreach

-To use personalized thumbnails

-To appeal copyright claims!

Verifying your account, as we said, is very easy. Just go to youtube.com/verify while logged into your Google account on your browser, and You will be taken to a page where you will be asked to specify your country of residence and how you want to verify your account.

First, select your country from the display menu. Most likely, your country will be set by default based on your IP address, but if not, choose the correct one from the menu. Below you will be asked whether you would like to receive the verification code by voice message or by a text message to your phone.

Check either option according to your preferences. You will then be asked to choose which language they should use when sending you the code. Once you choose, enter your phone number into the text box below the “what is your phone number?” prompt and click on “submit”.

Now depending on which delivery method you choose, a text or voice message will be sent to your phone. Enter the 6-digit verification code that was sent to you by Google in the text box and click on “submit”.

The page should immediately reload and show you a “verified” mark and a message that reads: “congratulations! Your YouTube account is now verified.”. Click on “continue” to access your newly verified account!
