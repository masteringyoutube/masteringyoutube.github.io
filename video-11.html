Quick-Top-Ranking Tricks by using Your Name or Business Name

You learned something awesome, which is the importance of ranking YouTube Live videos on Google and YouTube by using money keywords, but what if you could use that strategy to not only Top Rank your video but also your name?

If what you want in the beginning is to make yourself known as a YouTuber it will not be enough to only make one or two of your videos using keywords as shortcuts to fame: you have to use the popularity of your video to make your name popular before you can start using your name to make your videos popular!

That means that you must use your talent and the quality of your videos in your favor to get your name all over the place!

When you rank your name on top it becomes clear that what will become popular is not only your content but also your name, and the benefits of having your name on top include:

-Viewers will not only become fans of your videos, but also your fans

-You will become a celebrity

-Your name will become relevant in YouTube and Google searches, which means that your name will be a trending topic too!

-Your content will be associated with your name, which will automatically make you an authority!

-Your name will become your brand!

Another unseen benefit of using your name for launching a live event on YouTube is that your name will hardly face competition when it comes to ranking on top because most video titles contain keywords that are already in use, which means that your name will help you to rank on top!

You can also use this nice little trick with the name of your business, which will also give you the benefits of ranking your video on top and helping your business to reach a broader audience.

The catch is that the name of your business might need a little more optimization than your personal name.

That is because the name of your company more than likely shares something in common with the names of other companies.

To create a live event with your personal or business name, just go to your “creator studio” dashboard on YouTube and click on the “live streaming” tab, then on “event”.

Next click on “new live event”.

In the “basic info”, introduce your name or the name of your business as the name of your event.

Enter your name or the name of your business on each corresponding field for this to work.

Click on “go live now”.

Wait for everything to set up

Now simply go to either platform and type your name or the name of your business in the search bar.
