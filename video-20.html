How can you have someone else do the hard work for you?

How was it to finally launch your own show, hosted by yourself, and also directed and produced by yourself? Easy enough, right? Doing something that you love is in itself the greatest reward!

And what about setting up everything else you did? What was it like to find the right image for your channel? what was it like to build your social media presence and, most importantly, to properly engage people on your social profiles?

We could keep going on for the whole day, asking you about all the other stuff that you have to do besides creating your content, rehearsing, getting everything ready and testing. How much time did that suck away from you doing what you are really good at… live streaming?

Wouldn’t it be great to have a crew to give all that hard work to? well, we are glad to be here for you, as we will be showing you how to delegate every one of those tasks to someone else, someone who knows the trade inside out, leaving you with all the time to dedicate to creating big events!

Fiverr.com is great because there you will be able to find the right people that fit the skills that you need for everything related to your YouTube channel. Maybe now you don’t see the immediate need to find a crew, but you will down the road as your audience starts to grow and your shows start to get bigger and better!

There you will find “graphics and design” services for your profile pics, your banners, your preview screens and your thumbnails, “digital marketing” services for promotion, “writing and translation” services to get a helping hand with content creation and double checking.

“Video and animation” services for creating advanced and attention grabbing animations and effects for your presentations, “music and audio” services for sound effects or music production, among many others that will start appealing to you according to your needs!
